Shows off but for a reason. Artist, Designer.
An artist-designer is engaged in the original design of any objects. Designers literally make our life interesting and colorful. The main meaning of this profession is to give a new look to an existing thing. There are many types of artist-designer professions, some of them are: interior designer, fashion designer, web designer and landscape designer. The duties of an artist-designer, depending on the scope of his activity, include: the development of ideas and concepts in the form of visual graphic schemes, posters and layouts and the implementation of projects.
